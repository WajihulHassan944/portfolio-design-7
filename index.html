    About Me

    Phoebe Chok is a Medical Product Specialist, Model, Cookbook Author and a Digital Activist for Eating-Disorder Awareness in Brunei Darussalam.
    She is no stranger to the local fashion scene, as she had the opportunity of working together with a number of homegrown brands including Na Forrer, Ardly Raman, XOIA and Lisa Borhan. She has also worked with notable regional fashion-brands that include Jovian Mandagie and Women of Haya.
    She hopes to continue working with Healthcare Professionals to save patients' lives, to model regionally & internationally, and to collaborate with & support regional eating-disorder stakeholders, facilities and foundations. Hence, she aspires to bring in positive change, knowledge and acceptance towards eating-disorder & body-image awareness in Brunei.
    In her free time, she loves to ride horses, practice yoga and bake vegan desserts!
